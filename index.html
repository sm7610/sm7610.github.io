Shruti Mishra

I received a PhD in Applied Mathematics from Harvard University, where I worked on problems in continuum mechanics, computation and machine learning. I have spent some time learning and doing research at DeepMind, on reinforcement learning in continuous control environments, and at Mila, on the use of deep learning to forecast natural convection.
